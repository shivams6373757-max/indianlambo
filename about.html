Features of the Great Lamborghini

Lamborghini cars are renowned for their striking, futuristic designs, high-performance engines, and cutting-edge technology.
Key features include powerful, naturally aspirated V12 or V10 engines, often paired with advanced aerodynamics
like active rear wings and front splitters, enhancing downforce and stability.
Carbon fiber construction is prevalent, contributing to light weight and increased rigidity,
while features like Lamborghini's ALA (Aerodinamica Lamborghini Attiva) system and LRS (Lamborghini Rear-wheel Steering) further optimize handling and performance.
Inside, Lamborghini boasts luxurious interiors with high-quality materials, advanced infotainment systems, and customizable driving modes.
